You can also follow Super League each week on BBC TV with the Super League Show. Presented by Tanya Arnold plus top guests from the sport in the studio, the programme rounds up the best of the weekend action together with incisive comment and features on the game's top personalities.
The programme goes out every Monday on BBC One across the north of England.  If you are a satellite or cable viewer you can access these BBC regions via your EPG.
