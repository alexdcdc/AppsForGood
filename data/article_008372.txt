Romeo Nkansah, a Dutch national living in Holloway, was with friends when he was stabbed in the early hours of 29 May in Greenland Road, Camden Town.
A row broke out between the 37-year-old and a group of men, police said.
Met Police arrested the men, aged 21 and 19, on Thursday on suspicion of murder. They remain in custody.
Mr Nkansah was pronounced dead in the hospital. A post-mortem examination found he died from a single stab wound.
An altercation took place after the victim and his friends chatted to a group of women during the night out, police said.
Appealing for witnesses and information Det Ch Insp Jamie Piscopo said police want to speak with "two women in particular that we believe Romeo was trying to talk to".
