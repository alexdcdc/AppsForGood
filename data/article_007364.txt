Six canisters were set off in a section of the West Stand of Palmerston Park which housed visiting fans.
Ch Insp Stuart Davidson, the match commander, said inquiries were ongoing and CCTV footage was being reviewed.
The official match attendance was 2,358 - with about half made up of away fans of the side from Northern Ireland.
Police said there had been no arrests and only one person was ejected from the ground by stewards.
