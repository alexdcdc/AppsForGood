Overall winner was a new property called Strathdon House designed by Brown + Brown Architects and built near Strathdon in Aberdeenshire.
The overall runner up was an extension to a house called Tigh an Uillt in Nethybridge and entered into the competition by Sean and Lara Langmuir.
The contest celebrated new and longer established buildings and structures.
It also helped to mark Scotland's Year of Innovation, Architecture and Design 2016.
The full results are available on the Cairngorms National Park Authority website.
