Police Scotland said Abdelnabi Alainani, 30, was found in Scunthorpe on Saturday and was now in the care of Humberside Police and the NHS.
A major search operation was launched after Mr Alainani left the Midpark Hospital.
A lorry driver who travelled from Dumfries reported seeing a man getting out of his vehicle in Scunthorpe.
Police thanked the media and members of the public for their assistance during their inquires.
