The woman was assaulted as she walked on a pathway near Buckingham Road, Bletchley, at about 13:00 BST on Friday.
A man pushed her to the ground and attacked her before walking off.
The suspect is described as white and in his early to mid-20s, with a large build.
He was wearing a black beanie hat, black jacket and black trousers. He was carrying a black rucksack over both shoulders.
Police have appealed for witnesses.
