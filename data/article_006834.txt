France's Christelle Daunay was second in 33.08 with Britain's Katrina Wootton third in 33.18.
In the men's race, American Dathan Ritzenhein beat compatriot Bernard Lagat to take the title.
Ritzenhein made a strong start before pulling away towards the end to finish in 28 minutes and six seconds.
South African Stephen Mokoka finished third in 28.22, while Andy Vernon (28.26) was the highest placed Briton in sixth.
Highlights of the run will be shown on BBC Two at 17:00 BST on Sunday, 28 May.
