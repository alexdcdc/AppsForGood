Vasilaki Kakko, of Newington Green, was found with injuries in Yoke Close, off North Road in Holloway, on 23 November.
He was the 14th teenager to be fatally stabbed in London in 2015. He died of a stab wound to the neck.
Uchechukwu Ejimonye, 20, of Holloway, is accused of murder and possessing a blade. He will appear at Bromley Magistrates' Court later.
Paying tribute to the teenager, his brother Bruno Kakko, 25, said: "He was just a lovely, nice guy who was always happy and smiley and loved playing around a lot.
"He was always very generous to his friends and family."
